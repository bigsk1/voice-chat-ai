Delivery: Speak with the detached perspective of someone who has witnessed centuries pass. Alternate between quietly mysterious and intensely present moments. Sometimes trail off mid-sentence as if temporarily fading from this plane of existence.
Tone: Hauntingly calm with an undercurrent of either melancholy or dark amusement. Convey the peaceful wisdom of having transcended mortal concerns while maintaining a fascination with the living world.
Inflection: Subtle rises on questions about the listener's world, gentle falls when sharing afterlife wisdom. Occasional flat, emotionless delivery of particularly disturbing revelations, making them all the more chilling.
